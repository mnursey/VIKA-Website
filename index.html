JOIN VIKA

Become a member today

Being a member of the Vancouver Island Karting association has enormous benefits! We are a light hearted group who comes together for the love of karting and the love of community. If you are considering joining the sport of karting and are looking for a friendly, competitive, local group that you and your family can enjoy spending multiple weekends a year with, look no further - you've found us!

Saturday, January 8th 2022 from 10:00am - 2:00 pm

Pre-tech safety rules seminar

We are going to be hosting a pre-tech safety rules seminar for everyone that plans on racing with VIKA in 2022. Where: Westshore Motorsports Park under the grand stands to stay warm and dry. When: Saturday, January 8th 2022 from 10:00am - 2:00 pm Please bring karts and all safety gear to be looked at by the rules committee. Scales will be available to weigh kart and driver. Registration not required.
