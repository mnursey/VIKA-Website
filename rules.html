2023 Summer Series Rules

Our rules will follow the 2023 WCKC Rules with the following amendments:

  1. Motors that were given the engine claim rules in the 2023 WCKC rules, but did not have an engine claim rule previously will not have the engine claim rule applied. Motors with an engine claim rule in the 2022 and 2023 WCKC rules will continue to have a engine claim rule.

  2. Motors may use either leaded or non leaded fuel for the 2023 season.
